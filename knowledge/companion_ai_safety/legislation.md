# Companion AI Legislation

## US State Laws

### California SB 243 (effective Jan 1, 2026)
First state mandate for companion chatbot safety for minors:
- Prevent sexual content exposure to minors
- Notification that chatbots are AI
- Suicidal ideation protocol with crisis service referrals
- Annual reporting on chatbot use and suicidal ideation
- Private right of action: $5,000 per violation or 3x actual damages

Source: [Analysis](https://www.joneswalker.com/en/insights/blogs/ai-law-blog/ai-regulatory-update-californias-sb-243-mandates-companion-ai-safety-and-accoun.html)

### Minnesota (proposed 2026)
Complete ban on companion AI for under-18s.

Source: [MinnPost](http://www.minnpost.com/state-government/2026/03/citing-suicides-minnesota-lawmakers-want-to-end-minors-access-to-ai-companions/)

### Landscape

## US Federal

### GUARD Act (introduced Oct 2025)
Would ban minors from AI companion chatbots entirely. Age verification via government ID. Fine up to $100,000 per offense.

Source: [Congress.gov](https://www.congress.gov/bill/119th-congress/senate-bill/3062/text) | [Analysis](https://thebeckagefirm.com/the-guard-act-congress-moves-to-regulate-ai-chatbots-for-minors/)

### ENFORCE Act (passed Senate Dec 2025)
Prosecute producers of AI-modified CSAM whether or not intended for distribution. Pending House.

Source: [Thorn](https://www.thorn.org/blog/the-enforce-act-addressing-ai-generated-csam-offenses/)

### TAKE IT DOWN Act (signed May 2025)
Federal crime to publish non-consensual intimate imagery (real or AI-generated). 48-hour platform takedown. Enforcement May 19, 2026.

## EU AI Act

Companion AI regulation falls under the Act's provisions on AI systems that exploit vulnerabilities of specific groups (including minors and people in psychological distress) — classified as "unacceptable risk" and banned outright.

Enforcement timeline:
- Feb 2, 2025: Prohibitions took effect
- Aug 2, 2025: GPAI rules apply
- Aug 2, 2026: Most rules in force, national enforcement begins

Source: [EU AI Act Timeline](https://ai-act-service-desk.ec.europa.eu/en/ai-act/timeline/timeline-implementation-eu-ai-act)
