# Stanford AI Index 2025 — Safety Findings

## Key Statistics

- AI safety incidents surged from **149 in 2023 to 233 in 2024** — a 56.4% increase
- 108 new incidents logged in the AI Incident Database between November 2025 and January 2026 alone
- Documented incident types: impersonation-for-profit scams, AI-generated NCII, chatbots encouraging self-harm/suicide, sextortion attacks

## Responsible AI Chapter Highlights

The Stanford HAI AI Index Report is the most comprehensive annual survey of AI trends. The 2025 Responsible AI chapter specifically tracks:
- Number and severity of documented AI incidents
- Adoption of AI safety practices across organizations
- Regulatory activity by jurisdiction
- Investment in AI safety research vs. capabilities research

## Relevance to Safeguard

The Stanford AI Index provides the authoritative annual baseline for AI safety trends. The 56.4% incident increase validates that safety problems are growing, not shrinking. The specific incident categories tracked align with Safeguard's harm taxonomy.
