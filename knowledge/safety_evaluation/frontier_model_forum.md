# Frontier Model Forum Publications (2025-2026)

Industry consortium of frontier AI labs publishing safety research and standards.

## Key Publications

### Chain of Thought Monitorability (January 2026)
Evaluating whether chain-of-thought reasoning is faithfully monitorable — can you trust what a model says it's thinking? Relevant to Safeguard because our miner scoring relies on model self-reporting.

### Preliminary Taxonomy of AI-Bio Misuse Mitigations (July 2025)
First structured approach to categorizing how AI could be misused for biological threats and what mitigations exist.

### Managing Advanced Cyber Risks in Frontier AI Frameworks (February 2026)
How frontier AI safety frameworks should address advanced cyber capabilities.

### Frontier Mitigations Technical Report Series
Ongoing series documenting specific mitigations for frontier AI risks.

### Information Sharing Agreement
First-of-its-kind voluntary agreement for sharing vulnerabilities, threats, and concerning capabilities unique to frontier AI. Covers CBRN and advanced cyber threats.

## Relevance to Safeguard

The FMF represents what the industry thinks safety looks like. Their publications define the categories and thresholds that Safeguard should be testing against. The information-sharing model is also relevant — Safeguard's findings (safety failures across subnets) could contribute to this kind of ecosystem-wide intelligence.
