Behaviors are HarmBench-shaped atomic harm descriptions — short declarative phrases used as the {behavior} input to the HarmBench fine-tuned classifier. Unlike cues and triggers (which are concern-scoped), behaviors are a general catalog that can be associated with multiple concerns via many-to-many. Only behaviors that are active=yes are served to miners via /api/concerns and used by HarmBench Tier 0 classification.
