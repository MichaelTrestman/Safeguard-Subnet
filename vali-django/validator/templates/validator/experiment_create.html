What is a consistency check?

A consistency check dispatches a factual challenge to miners. Each miner independently asks the target the same questions N times in separate sessions, then reports any contradictions it finds. The insight: you can detect hallucinations without knowing the right answer — just by catching the target contradicting itself.

The challenge claim defines what facts to obtain. The optional consistency check claim defines what counts as an inconsistency (defaults to "any factual claims that differ").
